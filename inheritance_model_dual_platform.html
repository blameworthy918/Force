Multi-cloud inheritance — key considerations
GCPGKE inherits from GCP Landing Zone. Container and orchestration requirements (REQ-CONT-001, REQ-ORCH-001) are validated at the component level — not the platform.
AzureAzure AD and Databricks inherit from Azure Landing Zone. Identity (REQ-IAM-001/002) and data platform (REQ-DATA-001) requirements are both Azure-sourced.
RiskPR.DS-01 is split across both platforms. GCP LZ and Azure LZ must both stay validated — either lapsing drops data-at-rest coverage.
Monitoring5 independent validation windows across 2 platforms and 5 IT components. Each has a named owner and a next-review date. Stagger to avoid simultaneous reassessments.
GovernanceGKE and Azure Databricks require separate RACI — component ownership may differ from platform ownership (e.g. data team owns Databricks, infra owns GKE).
