⚡ Change impact — review-frequency: quarterly → semi-annual
1
Update review-frequency: semi-annual in this Fact Sheet. Bump version to v1.1.
2
LeanIX shows all governed requirements via the relation — REQ-IAM-001, REQ-IAM-002, REQ-IAM-005 are immediately visible.
3
Each requirement owner is notified. Requirements referencing the changed parameter get versioned (v1.0 → v1.1).
4
OSCAL export picks up the new set-parameter value automatically from the updated Fact Sheet. No manual OSCAL editing.
💡 How many Company Standard Fact Sheets?
Group by topic, not by control. A Moderate baseline has 300+ controls and 500+ parameters — one Fact Sheet per parameter is unmanageable. Instead: IAM Account Standards (all AC-family account parameters) · Encryption Standards (SC-family key lengths, algorithms, CMEK requirements) · Audit & Logging Standards (AU-family retention periods, alert thresholds) · Incident Response Standards (IR-family notification windows, escalation paths). Aim for 12–20 Company Standard Fact Sheets covering the full Moderate baseline. Related parameters change together — grouping by topic matches how your org actually makes policy decisions.
