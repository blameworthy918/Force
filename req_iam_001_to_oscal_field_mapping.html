Versioning — REQ-IAM-001.v1 → v2: The human ID (REQ-IAM-001) stays constant. A new UUID is generated for v2 and stored in LeanIX. The v1 UUID is marked superseded in OSCAL metadata. Systems assessed against v1 stay on v1 until they re-certify against v2. This is why storing the UUID in LeanIX from day one matters — retrofitting UUIDs across hundreds of requirements is painful.
